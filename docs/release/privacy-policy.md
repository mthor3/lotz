# Lotz Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Lotz is an offline lottery-strategy simulator. It is for exploring simulated odds and long-run outcomes; it does not sell lottery tickets, accept wagers, or predict winning numbers.

## Data collection

Lotz does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. It does not require an account and does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, or third-party data collection SDKs.

Simulation inputs and results are used only while the app is running. The current version does not persist simulation data after the app is closed.

## Children

Lotz is not directed to children. Because the app collects no personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version adds data collection, online features, accounts, analytics, or advertising, this policy will be updated before that version is released.

## Contact

For questions about this policy or the app, open an issue at [github.com/mthor3/lotz/issues](https://github.com/mthor3/lotz/issues).
